summary of internet:-

-> In 1969, the American Department of Defense (DOD) started a network of devices
called ARPANET (Advanced Research Project Administration Network).

-> The Internet, referred to as the Net,consists of a complex network of computers
connected by high-speed communication technologies.

-> Functioning of the internet is not controlled by any one perticular organisation.
It is managed by a group of voluntary organisations.

-> Rules for communication over the internet are called as protocals.

-> The electronics pages seen on the Internet are knows as web pages.
